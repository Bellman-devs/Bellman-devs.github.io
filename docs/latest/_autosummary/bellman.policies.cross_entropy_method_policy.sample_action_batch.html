bellman.policies.cross_entropy_method_policy.sample_action_batch¶

bellman.policies.cross_entropy_method_policy.sample_action_batch(mean, var, low, high, batch_size)[source]¶

Sample a batch of trajectories of actions from a truncated normal distribution with parameters mean and var.

Parameters

  • mean (Tensor) – The mean of the truncated normal distribution.

  • var (Tensor) – The variance of the truncated normal distribution.

  • low (Tensor) – The lower bound on the distribution’s support.

  • high (Tensor) – The upper bound on the distribution’s support.

  • batch_size (int) – The environment batch size.

Return type

Tensor

Returns

A batch of trajectories of actions.
